Info

If you have been to one of the DrupalCamps Ruhr in 2023 or 2018, this one will work basically the same.

If you have never been to one: DrupalCamp Ruhr is a two-day event that brings together developers and designers that work with Drupal. There will be sessions, discussions, networking, food, and drinks. It doesn’t matter if are a veteran Drupalista or new to Drupal. You will find enough interesting topics to learn something new.

As soon as the new website is online (by the end of May) you can register here and propose a session. Ideally, most people will do this a few months/weeks in advance. We will collect the sessions and group similar topics together.

But just like last time, there will be a Barcamp component to it. Every morning you may present a new session or topic for discussion which we will incorporate into the session plan. This often happens on Saturdays after some discussions on Friday.

We know that some of the participants come from other countries. So the event will be mostly in English. And if you have a session or discussion in mind we prefer it to be held in English. But, if you are uncomfortable to speak in English, you may offer the session in German instead. Usual topics include Site Building, Modules, Module Development, Frontend, Accessibility, and Performance. See the proposed sessions of DrupalCamp Ruhr 2023. The sessions will not be recorded.

Tickets

Tickets are available at Eventbrite. There are three options for basic tickets:

  • Early Bird: 29 Euro (till 30 June)
  • Regular: 39 Euro
  • Supporter: 69 Euro

If you want, you can grab a ticket that comes with a T-shirt (check out the info below).

  • Early Bird with T-Shirt: 39 Euro (till 30 June)
  • Regular with T-Shirt: 49 Euro (till 22 August)
  • Supporter with T-Shirt: 79 Euro (till 22 August)

The tickets are for both days of the DrupalCamp. There will not be tickets for a single day.

Some context on the prices: Every person will have free entry and food in the Unperfekthaus. This will cost us 82 Euro per person for two days. Any shirt we print will cost around 18 Euro (Premium Bio T-Shirts from Spreadshirt).
So a two-day ticket bundled with a shirt will cost us 100 Euro. And with Early Bird you can have both for just 39 Euro. What a deal!

But if you can and want to support us, we have added Supporter tickets this time around. Which are still a good deal.

If you plan to hold a session, thank you very much, but you still have to pay for ticket and shirt. The only exceptions are the free tickets/shirts for the Sponsors and the Featured Speakers.

Location

It can be quite difficult to find a decent location for events like this. We are in the fortunate position of having the Unperfekthus right here in Essen. This will be the seventh DrupalCamp Ruhr. And the seventh time we are meeting in the Unperfekthaus.

It’s hard to describe what exactly makes the Unperfekthaus the special place it is, home to artists and businesspeople alike. A well-known venue for meetings and gatherings of all sorts, there are a great many people and styles that amalgamate and make it truly unique.

Just like in 2023, we will have three rooms on the fourth floor. So we can have three sessions at the same time. Smaller groups can find additional spaces within the Unperfekthaus for BOFs or smaller discussions. We will also use room 222 for the Nerd Quiz on Friday evening.

Data Privacy

This is a purely static website without cookies or forms. No server logs are generated either.

We are counting visitors with Matomo. IP addresses are anonymised, i.e. the last two bytes of the address are masked. We cannot assign these IP addresses to individual persons. Matomo is configured so that it does not set any cookies. We are just interested in the general ballpark of visitors.
